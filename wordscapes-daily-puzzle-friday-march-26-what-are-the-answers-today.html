WORDSCAPES is one of the most popular games available to download on iOS and android.

What is the Wordscapes daily puzzle?

Wordscapes is a word puzzle video game created by the American studio PeopleFun.

It has been available on both the Google Play Store, and the Apple App Store.

“Playing Wordscapes 10 mins a day sharpens your mind and prepares you for your daily life and challenges!” reads the Apple Store description.

“This text twist of a word game is tremendous brain challenging fun.

“Enjoy modern word puzzles with the best of word searching, anagrams, and crosswords!

“Immerse yourself into the beautiful scenery backgrounds to relax and ease your mind.”

How do you earn coins in Wordscapes?

There are several ways you can earn Wordscapes coins.

You can get them by playing bonus words, filling in the extra word on some puzzles, collecting stars, and leveling up in the game.
