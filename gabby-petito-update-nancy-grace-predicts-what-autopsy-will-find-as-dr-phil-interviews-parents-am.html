VINE TATTOOS ON GABBY, JOE AND BRIAN

Gabby’s father Joe also sported hand tattoos, which matched a design Gabby and fiancé Brian Laundrie both wore.

The couple showed the coordinating designs on Instagram, a set of two vines that wrapped around their fingers. 

Gabby’s leaves were a black outline, with negative space left in the leaves, while Brians’ design is filled in.

According to TattooSEO, vine tattoos represent loyalty and a desire to stick by someone’s side.

Last year, Brian posted a photo of himself and Gabby, which included the caption “Till death do us part or until I wake up, I’m so happy the answer was yes.”
