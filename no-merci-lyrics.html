No Merci Lyrics

August 2024 · 4 minute read

No Merci is a song by English rapper Little Simz, from her fifth studio album No Thank You, released on December 12th, 2022.

The song is produced by Inflo and features a sample of Requiem Pour Un Con by French singer Serge Gainsbourg.

No Merci lyrics express Simz’s confidence and independence as an artist in the music industry,

The song also showcases Simz’s rap skills and witty wordplay.
