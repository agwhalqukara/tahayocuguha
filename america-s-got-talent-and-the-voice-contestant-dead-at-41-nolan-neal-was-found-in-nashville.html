America's Got Talent and The Voice contestant dead at 41: Nolan Neal was found in Nashville

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Singer Nolan Neal has died at the age of 41.

The musician was best known as a contestant on two hot competition TV shows: America’s Got Talent where he worked with Howie Mandel, Heidi Klum, Sofía Vergara and Simon Cowell and The Voice where he was on Adam Levine’s team.

The crooner was found dead in his Nashville apartment on Monday, shared his cousin Dylan Seals with TMZ. The site also confirmed the death with the medical examiner in Nashville.
