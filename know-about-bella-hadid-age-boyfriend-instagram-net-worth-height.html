Know About Bella Hadid; Age, Boyfriend, Instagram, Net Worth, Height

July 2024 · 3 minute read

The on-and-off relationship of Bella Hadid and her singer boyfriend often hits the media surface. However, the stunning model seldom shares her relationship status on her social site, i.e. Instagram.

Bella Hadid is an American model, who has been signed to IMG Models in 2014. She became the “Model of the Year” for Model.com’s Model of the Year 2016 Awards by the Industry.

Bella Hadid Bio; Early Life, Parents, Height

Isabella Khair Hadid was born on 9 October 1996 in Washington, D.C. Her parents are real-estate developer Mohamed Hadid and former model Yolanda Hadid. She grew up with two siblings, an older sister Gigi Hadid, and a younger brother, Anwar Hadid in Los Angeles, California.

Bella with a height of 5 feet 9 inches (1.75 meters) studied photography at the Parsons School of Design. However, she dropped out of school because of her interest in modeling.

Her Modeling Career; Net Worth

After signing to IMG Models age of 18 on 21 August 2014, she first appeared in the New York Fashion Week walking for Desigual.  She was featured on the cover of magazines such as Jalouse Magazine, Seventeen, Vogue and V Magazine.

Moreover, Hadid won the Model of the Year in both Second Annual Fashion Los Angeles Awards and GQ Men of the Year Awards in 2015. In addition, she is the brand ambassador of various top brands such as Dior Beauty, Bulgari, Nike, and TAG Heuer.

Bella Hadid walks the Runway at Fashion during the 71st annual Cannes Film Festival at Aeroport Cannes Mandelieu in France in May 2018 (Picture: Zimbio)

As a young model, Bella is making big money from her association with top brands by her appearance in fashion shows. Currently, she enjoys the live lifestyle with an estimated net worth of $12 million.

Bella Hadid’s Boyfriend; The Weeknd

The 22-year-old is currently dating Canadian singer The Weeknd aka Abel Makkonen Tesfaye. The couple has been together since April 2015 but reportedly split in November 2016.

Again, they got back together in July 2018 and has been inseparable since then. Despite their romantic relationship, they seldom share the glimpse of their romance on their Instagram.

Bella Hadid and The Weeknd started dating in April 2015 (Picture: Cosmopolitan)

In March 2019, Bella shared a series of pictures, kissing The Weeknd on her Instagram. The pair are currently enjoying a low-profile relationship so far.

Some Interesting Facts About Bella Hadid

  • Bella is a practicing Muslim and is proud of her father’s heritage.
  • She had dreams of attending the 2016 Olympics in Rio de Janeiro but had to give up competing in 2013 because of suffering from chronic Lyme disease.
  • Her mother is of Dutch descent, and her father is Palestinian.
  • Both her siblings Gigi and Anwar are fashion models.
