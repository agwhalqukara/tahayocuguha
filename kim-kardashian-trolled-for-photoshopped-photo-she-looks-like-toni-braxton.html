Kim Kardashian Trolled For Photoshopped Photo She Looks Like Toni Braxton

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Kim Kardashian shared new photos as a way of promoting her upcoming KKW Beauty products, the Matte Smoke and Matte Cocoa Collections but the photos aren’t having the intended response with viewers. While Kim was supposed to be showing off her makeup, others were accusing her of photoshopping the images to the point they say they no longer looked like Kim, but looked more like Toni Braxton! The comments were fast and furious and while many of Kim’s celebrity friends praised the look, the comments were giving a different vibe entirely.

The majority of fans and followers didn’t respond by praising the makeup but stayed on the theme that Kim had gone overboard with photoshop. Kim shared the following message promoting the new makeup.

“I’m so excited to announce my new 90’s inspired collection of all matte formulas- The Matte Smoke & Matte Cocoa Collections!!! I get a lot of my makeup inspiration from the 90’s and love how nostalgic this entire collection is. The collection features 2 10-Pan Eyeshadow Palettes, 6 Lipsticks in a brand new matte formula, 6 Lip Liners, and 5 Eyeliners. Matte Smoke & Matte Cocoa are two entirely different color ranges, so there are so many looks you can create with this collection. I can’t wait for you guys to try it!!! Launching to kkwbeauty.com on Friday, 08.16 at 12pm pst #kkwbeauty.”

You may see the photos Kim Kardashian shared that were compared to Toni Braxton below.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B0y5n0oAk7h/

Seemingly unfazed by the negative response, Kim shared another photo where people found she looked unrecognizable.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B0003wsA2gt/

When reading comments (and there are more than 12,000 of them) you’ll find that celebrities with a blue checkmark left positive responses, but as soon as those end and regular folk weigh in, the negative remarks abound. Some of the comments simply said she no longer looked like herself. One commenter said, “This is why Beyonce doesn’t like you.” And another person wrote, “Did you borrow Khloe’s nose for this photo?”
