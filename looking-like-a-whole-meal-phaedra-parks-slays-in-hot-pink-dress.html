Looking Like a Whole Meal: Phaedra Parks Slays in Hot Pink Dress

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Phaedra Parks wasn’t included in the new “The Real Housewives of Atlanta” cast photo where everyone totally slayed in black, but she hit the town in hot pink all on her own.

The outsted peach holder stepped out Wednesday night to ring in rapper T.I.’s 39th birthday at The Brasserie at Bazati restaurant in Atlanta.

“#Aboutlastnight celebrating a 👑 G-day @troubleman31 🎂” the Atlanta-based attorney captioned a series of photos of her arriving at the bash and rubbing elbows with Tip and his wife Tiny Harris.

Parks wore her ombré blond hair draped around her shoulders as she strutted in a hot pink ruffled Victor Glemaud dress. She paired the look with simple jewelry, strappy gold sandal heels and a Louis Vuitton beauty case.

The look instantly dazzled fans who couldn’t wait to pile on more than 900 comments about the outfit.

Parks has been keeping busy over the past few months — especially when it comes to her love life.

The ex-Bravolebrity recently made things Instagram official with her boyfriend Medina Islam, who stars in the OWN series “The Haves and the Have Nots.” The moment came after Parks was photographed hand-in-hand with her beau — who is about 10 years her junior — when they grabbed lunch together at The Misfit Restaurant and Bar in California in July.

And while the romance is still relatively new, the pair could be preparing to take it to the next level, if a report from TMZ is to be believed. Parks was reported to be gearing up to return to reality TV by joining the upcoming season of We TV’s “Marriage Boot Camp.” The new couple is apparently navigating the next phase of their relationship, and Parks is looking to live in Los Angeles, where Medina is based. As such, Parks is mulling uprooting her sons Ayden, 9, and Dylan, 6, to be closer to her boyfriend.

Parks is still tending to her young sons, whom she shares with ex-husband Apollo Nida, who was recently released from prison after serving five years on charges of conspiring to commit mail, wire and bank fraud. Parks recently saw off her boys to the first day of school after reflecting on their summer days at the beach.
